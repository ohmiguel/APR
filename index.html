Nous avons le plaisir et la joie de vous accueillir à l'Assemblée Pentecôtiste de Rennes (APR).
Ceci est un lieu de repos, de restauration et de sortie.

We believe that community is the very heart beat of God; therefore here at APR we centre ministry and service to one another on the concept of community.  It is within this community that we strive to grow in Christ as we learn to trust our everlasting and ever loving Father.

We also desire to build Christian character in both good and bad times, as iron sharpens iron. In difficult seasons, which are bound to come in the life of a believer, we find comfort and encouragement within this community. Through the teaching of the word and fellowship with one another, we are equipped and trained to deal effectively with life's challenges, learn how to relate to God and ultimately become all He created us to be.

Finally in community, we all stand in awe as we watch the workings of the Holy Spirit, who always does beyond what we can ask for or imagine.

Our prayer is that you'll find your home here as we journey together.
 
With Love,
